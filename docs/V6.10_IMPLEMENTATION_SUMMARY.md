## What's Being Updated

### 1. **Time-Aware Alerts (Your Main Request)**

- Queued alerts deliver at 7:00 AM batch
- Critical VIX spike alerts bypass quiet hours

### 2. **12-Point Scoring System (Pine v6.10 Parity)**
Current: 9-point max → New: 12-point max
- P1: HTF Trend (3 pts) ← was 1 pt
- P2: Momentum (2 pts) ← MACD + RSI combined
- P3: Volume (3 pts) ← was 1 pt
- P4: Regime (2 pts) ← TRENDING/VOLATILE only
- P5: Candle Body (2 pts) ← **NEW** — body > 50% of bar

### 3. **Circuit Breaker**
After 3 consecutive losses:
- Pause new signals for 10 bars (30 min on 30m chart)
- 4th loss: +10 bars (60 min total)
- 5th loss: +10 bars (90 min total)
- Prevents repeated-loss chains

### 4. **Dynamic Regime TP/SL**
**VOLATILE Regime:**
- TP1: 1.5R (was 1.0R) — ride momentum
- TP2: 2.5R (was 2.0R)
- TP3: 4.0R (was 3.5R)
- SL: 2.5× ATR (was 2.0× ATR) — avoid noise stops

**QUIET Regime:**
- TP1: 0.8R (was 1.0R) — grab profit fast
- TP2: 1.5R (was 2.0R)
- TP3: 2.0R (was 3.5R)
- SL: 1.5× ATR — tighter

## Config Changes Required

Add to `symbols.yaml`:
```yaml
settings:
  scanner:
    time_filter:
      enabled: true
      quiet_start: "22:00"  # 10 PM ET
      quiet_end: "07:00"    # 7 AM ET
      batch_delivery: true  # Queue alerts for 7 AM
      
    circuit_breaker:
      enabled: true
      loss_threshold: 3     # Pause after 3 losses
      cooldown_bars: 10     # 30 min on 30m TF
      
    dynamic_rr:
      enabled: true         # Adjust TP/SL by regime
      volatile_tp3: 4.0     # Wider targets
      quiet_tp3: 2.0        # Tighter targets
```
